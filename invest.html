Strategies of investing in crypto



  1. Strategy 1 (buy and hold strategy)

    2. The best crypto investment approach is to purchase and hold. Many crypto investors call this approach 'HODL'
    HODLing means buying a digital asset and keeping it in your digital wallet for a long time. Choosing the buy and hold method means you won't be trading much bitcoin. You just purchase coins and store them.
    If you don't intend on selling any of your assets for years, the HODL method may be right for you, especially if you're aiming to establish a nest egg for your kids.


  2. Strategy 2 (earning a yield)

    3. Another common way to invest in crypto is to "earn a yield." Using this strategy, you buy crypto and then hold on to it for a set amount of time to make money.

    In order to earn a yield, you only keep your crypto for a certain amount of time. In order to HODL, you keep your crypto for a long time. Buy coins at one price and then sell them for more money.

    A good thing about this investing strategy is that you can often make money while you own things. This is like how a savings account pays you interest on your balance.

  3. Strategy 3 (Dollar cost averaging)

    4. It involves investing set sums of money into cryptocurrency at regular periods.

    This technique relies on the fact that the crypto market is volatile, making timing difficult. Investing modest sums regularly should potentially reduce risk while increasing market exposure.

    Dollar cost averaging spreads your assets over time to protect you from price fluctuations. That implies your investment will pay off over time.

    5. Most assets are undervalued, meaning their real value is more than what they are trading for. If you've heard of investor Warren Buffet, he's the man who popularised value investing.
    Value investing is all about identifying undervalued assets. You'd then buy the asset, expecting it to appreciate in value over time. The main problem is determining whether assets are really cheap. Profiting from this method needs a lot of study and experience.
